Favorite Food

One of my absolute favorites — Tortilla Española (or Spanish Tortilla). I love it served cold as it pairs well with almost every beverage that way.

My go-to is making it with really good extra-virgin olive oil, thinly sliced potatoes, and an onion. Once set, I let it chill in the fridge. Goes great with arugala-based salads.
